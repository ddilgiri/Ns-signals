PART 1 — Upload files to GitHub (free online storage)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 1: Create GitHub account
  1. Open Chrome on your phone
  2. Go to https://github.com
  3. Tap "Sign up"
  4. Enter email, password, username
  5. Verify your email (check inbox, click link)

STEP 2: Create a new repository (online folder)
  1. After login, tap "+" icon (top right)
  2. Tap "New repository"
  3. Name: nse-signals
  4. Select: Private  (IMPORTANT — keep files private!)
  5. Check "Add a README file"
  6. Tap "Create repository"

STEP 3: Extract the zip file on your phone
  1. Find the downloaded zip in your Downloads folder
  2. Tap it → Tap "Extract" or "Extract here"
  3. A folder appears with all the files

STEP 4: Upload ALL files to GitHub
  1. In your new GitHub repo, tap "Add file" → "Upload files"
  2. Tap "choose your files"
  3. Select ALL these files from the extracted folder:
       server.js
       index.html
       package.json
       package-lock.json
       manifest.json
       sw.js
       icon-192.png
       icon-512.png
       Procfile
       railway.json
       render.yaml
  4. Tap "Commit changes" (green button)
  5. Done! All files are online now.


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
PART 2 — Run the server FREE on Railway (never sleeps)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 5: Create Railway account
  1. Go to https://railway.app
  2. Tap "Start a New Project"
  3. Tap "Login with GitHub"
  4. Tap "Authorize railway-app"
  5. You are logged in!

STEP 6: Deploy your server
  1. Tap "New Project"
  2. Tap "Deploy from GitHub repo"
  3. Find and tap "nse-signals"
  4. Tap "Deploy Now"
  5. Wait 2-3 minutes for it to build...
  6. You will see "Active" or "Success" when done.

STEP 7: Get your app URL
  1. Click your deployed service in Railway
  2. Go to "Settings" tab
  3. Under "Domains" tap "Generate Domain"
  4. You get a URL like:
       https://nse-signals-production.up.railway.app

  SAVE THIS URL — you need it in the next step!


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
PART 3 — Install the app on your Android phone
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 8: Open in Chrome
  1. Open Google Chrome on your Android
  2. Go to your Railway URL + /index.html
     Example: https://nse-signals-production.up.railway.app/index.html
  3. The app loads!

STEP 9: Install on home screen
  1. Chrome shows "Add to Home Screen" banner at bottom
     → Tap "Install"
  OR
  1. Tap Chrome menu (three dots top right)
  2. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  3. Tap "Add"
  4. App icon appears on your home screen!
  5. Open it — runs full screen like a real app.

STEP 10: Connect Angel One
  1. Open the app
  2. Tap "Setup" tab (bottom, plug icon)
  3. Server URL is filled automatically
  4. Enter your Angel One details:

     Client ID    = Your Angel One login ID (e.g. A123456)

     SmartAPI Key = Get FREE from smartapi.angelbroking.com
                    Sign in → My Apps → Create App → Copy Key

     Password     = Your Angel One trading password

     TOTP Secret  = Optional. Skip if you don't know this.

  5. Tap "Connect Angel One"
  6. You see "Connected — A123456"

STEP 11: Start scanning!
  1. Tap "Signals" tab (lightning bolt icon)
  2. Tap "SCAN" button (top right)
  3. Live F&O signals appear!
  4. LIVE badge = real price from Angel One
     EST badge = estimated (when market is closed)
  5. Tap any signal = see full P&L details

STEP 12: Enable notifications
  1. Tap "Setup" tab
  2. Tap "Enable Signal Notifications"
  3. Tap "Allow"
  4. You get alerts on your phone for every new signal!

App not loading?
  Wait 30 seconds — Railway may be starting up
  Check your URL is correct in Chrome

Not connected error?
  Go to Setup tab, enter credentials again, tap Connect
  Sessions expire after 8 hours — just reconnect

Premium shows EST not LIVE?
  Market must be open: 9:15 AM to 3:30 PM, Mon to Fri
  Reconnect Angel One and start scan again

Railway hours used up?
  Free hours reset on 1st of each month
  Add a card to get more free hours (no charge unless
  you manually upgrade to a paid plan)
